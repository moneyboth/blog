Making money is a skill. I'm convinced you can go from making $0 a day to $100K if you put in the work. But it comes down to how you approach it. Taking the time to learn a this skill and apply it is what matters most. Let's start with the basics: 1. What are you good at? 2. What market do you want to serve? 3. What are you interested in most? It's not a question of passion it's a question of 'interest'. Taking the time to truly know yourself and what you like or dislike. Could you site behind a computer for 8 hours? Or do you want to be in the outside?
